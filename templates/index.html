BLACKJACK

This is a Blackjack game made by using Flask, Python, JavaScript, jQueries, HTML, CSS, and SASS. The game is entirely based on points rather than actual money. Players will take turns to play against the house. If the player wins, he/she will be awarded a point, otherwise the next player in line will take his/her turn. The scores can be tracked live on the dedicated scoreboard. By the end of the final round, the scores are counted and the player with the highest score will be declared as the winner.

Before starting the game, please take a glance at the quick guide to this particular game to familiarise yourself with how the functions.

Options

Players sets the numbers of players. Up to four people can play at the same time. Decks sets the number of the decks in each play. Increasing the number of decks will make it fairly difficult to count cards. The highest number of decks allowed is 5. Rounds sets the numbers of rounds per session. The lower the rounds the shorter the game and vice versa. There is no limit for number of rounds to be played.

Quick Guide

Blackjack may be played with one to eight decks of 52-card decks. The cards 2 through 10 are worth their face value. Kings, queens, and jacks are each worth 10, and aces may be used as either 1 or 11. The object for the player is to draw cards totalling closer to 21, without going over, than the dealer's cards. The best total of all is a two-card 21, or a blackjack.
